# 🌾 GoviCONNECT: Smart Agriculture Platform & AI-Based Disease Diagnostic System

**GoSCONNECT** is a centralized digital ecosystem developed to support the small-holder farming community in Sri Lanka. By integrating AI-powered plant disease diagnostics with a direct marketplace and localized advisory services, the platform empowers farmers to reduce crop wastage and enhance financial stability.

---

## 🚀 Key Modules & Features

### 1. 🛠️ Admin Management Dashboard
The administrative hub for system governance and moderation:
* **User & Expert Control:** Manage all registered users and agricultural experts within the system.
* **Ad Moderation:** Oversee and manage sellers' advertisements in the marketplace.
* **System Oversight:** Monitor all platform activities and generate comprehensive reports.

### 2. 📰 Content Management System (CMS) & News Hub
A dedicated space for knowledge sharing and real-time updates:
* **Expert Blogs:** Tools for admins and experts to create, update, and delete agricultural blogs focusing on farming tips, disease prevention, and seasonal advice.
* **Community Interaction:** Allows farmers to read, comment on posts, and filter content for relevant information.
* **Live Global News:** Integrated feed fetching live agricultural news from **agweb.com**, providing direct links to full articles for up-to-date industry trends.

### 3. 🔍 AI Plant Disease Detection
The core intelligence module designed to solve early disease identification struggles:
* **CNN Diagnostics:** Utilizes Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) models to provide instant diagnostics from leaf images.
* **Treatment Mapping:** Automatically maps detected diseases to a rule-based knowledge system for specific prevention and treatment advice.

### 4. 🛒 Marketplace (Govi Mart)
A transparent trading platform for agricultural products:
* **Direct Selling:** Sellers can list products with pricing, categories, and shop details.
* **Buyer Tools:** Features for browsing items, contacting sellers directly, and reviewing/rating products.

### 5. 🛡️ Smart Crop Advisory System
A decision-support tool to maximize yield:
* **Personalized Recommendations:** Suggests suitable crops based on location, season, and crop type.
* **Care Instructions:** Provides guidelines for basic care, common diseases, and fertilizer usage.

### 6. 💬 Community Forum & Support
Fostering communication between farmers and experts:
* **Discussion Forum:** An open space for farming-related questions and community support.
* **AI Assistance:** Integration of Large Language Models (LLM) to provide quick suggested responses for common agricultural queries.

### 7. 📅 Personal Farming Workspace
* **Secure Access:** Role-based access control (User, Expert, Admin) with secure authentication.
* **Activity Tracker:** A digital calendar for tracking personal farming tasks and seasonal milestones.
