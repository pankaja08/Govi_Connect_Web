[cite_start]**GoSCONNECT** is a centralized digital ecosystem developed to support the small-holder farming community in Sri Lanka[cite: 21]. [cite_start]By integrating AI-powered plant disease diagnostics with a direct marketplace and localized advisory services, the platform empowers farmers to reduce crop wastage and enhance financial stability[cite: 23, 25].

---

## 🚀 Key Modules & Features

### 1. 🛠️ Admin Management Dashboard
[cite_start]The administrative hub for system governance and moderation[cite: 37]:
* [cite_start]**User & Expert Control:** Manage all registered users and agricultural experts within the system[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**Ad Moderation:** Oversee and manage sellers' advertisements in the marketplace[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**System Oversight:** Monitor all platform activities and generate comprehensive reports[cite: 37].

### 2. 📰 Content Management System (CMS) & News Hub
[cite_start]A dedicated space for knowledge sharing and real-time updates[cite: 37]:
* [cite_start]**Expert Blogs:** Tools for admins and experts to create, update, and delete agricultural blogs focusing on farming tips, disease prevention, and seasonal advice[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**Community Interaction:** Allows farmers to read, comment on posts, and filter content for relevant information[cite: 37].
* **Live Global News:** Integrated feed fetching live agricultural news from **agweb.com**, providing direct links to full articles for up-to-date industry trends.

### 3. 🔍 AI Plant Disease Detection
[cite_start]The core intelligence module designed to solve early disease identification struggles[cite: 27]:
* [cite_start]**CNN Diagnostics:** Utilizes Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) models to provide instant diagnostics from leaf images[cite: 28, 45].
* [cite_start]**Treatment Mapping:** Automatically maps detected diseases to a rule-based knowledge system for specific prevention and treatment advice[cite: 28, 45].

### 4. 🛒 Marketplace (Govi Mart)
[cite_start]A transparent trading platform for agricultural products[cite: 29, 37]:
* [cite_start]**Direct Selling:** Sellers can list products with pricing, categories, and shop details[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**Buyer Tools:** Features for browsing items, contacting sellers directly, and reviewing/rating products[cite: 37].

### 5. 🛡️ Smart Crop Advisory System
[cite_start]A decision-support tool to maximize yield[cite: 24, 37]:
* [cite_start]**Personalized Recommendations:** Suggests suitable crops based on location, season, and crop type[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**Care Instructions:** Provides guidelines for basic care, common diseases, and fertilizer usage[cite: 37].

### 6. 💬 Community Forum & Support
[cite_start]Fostering communication between farmers and experts[cite: 42]:
* [cite_start]**Discussion Forum:** An open space for farming-related questions and community support[cite: 42].
* [cite_start]**AI Assistance:** Integration of Large Language Models (LLM) to provide quick suggested responses for common agricultural queries[cite: 42].

### 7. 📅 Personal Farming Workspace
* [cite_start]**Secure Access:** Role-based access control (User, Expert, Admin) with secure authentication[cite: 37].
* [cite_start]**Activity Tracker:** A digital calendar for tracking personal farming tasks and seasonal milestones[cite: 29, 42].
