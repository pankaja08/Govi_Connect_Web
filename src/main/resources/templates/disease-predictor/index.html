        Tips for Precision Results

        Ensure the highest accuracy by following these imaging best practices.

        HD

        GOVI

        Daylight Only

        Early morning or late afternoon natural light provides the best texture visibility without harsh shadows.

        Plain Background

        Place the leaf on a solid white or gray surface to help the AI focus solely on the plant tissue patterns.

        Macro Focus

        Keep the device 10-15cm away. Ensure the lesion (diseased part) is sharp and in the center of the frame.

        No Multi-Leaf

        Avoid capturing multiple leaves, weeds, or hands in the background to prevent analysis confusion.
