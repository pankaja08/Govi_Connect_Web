Understanding Crop Performance Scoring

The Crop Performance charts represent the top-performing crops for each agricultural season based on our intelligent **Performance Scorer (CPS)**.

Formula:
CPS = (Total Income / Total Acres) × (Total Yield / Total Acres)
* Combines profitability per acre with yield productivity.
