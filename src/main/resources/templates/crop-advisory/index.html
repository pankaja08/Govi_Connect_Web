How It Works

Three simple steps to smarter farming decisions

1

Choose Your Location

Select your farming zone — Dry Zone, Intermediate Zone, or Wet Zone. This determines which crops are naturally suited to your climate and rainfall patterns.

2

Select Season & Soil Type

Tell us the current season (Maha or Yala) and your soil type. Soil type is optional — if unsure, select "Any" to see all compatible crops.

3

Get Detailed Results

Click "Get My Recommendations" to view a full page of matching crops — complete with images, care instructions, fertilizer guidance, and disease warnings.
